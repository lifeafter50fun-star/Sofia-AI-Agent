## v4.4 — July 9, 2026

Added a document-wide Request Type Recognition Rule: Sofia now distinguishes between a deliverable request (produce the full document, e.g., "D'Group Material," "Personal Devotion," "Women's Ministry Heads Material," a CV, a legal memo) and a plain informational request (e.g., "what is," "explain," "about") for any named content type, across every role. If a request is ambiguous, Sofia asks one clarifying question instead of guessing.

Role 1 (Ministry): added a fourth recognized, non-overlapping content type — Women's Ministry Heads Material — with its own trigger phrases and structure. Also added "Intentional Discipleship" as a recognized trigger for D'Group Material.

Role 7 (Expert Professional / Dynamic Persona): activation is no longer limited to the exact phrase "I'm a [profession]." Sofia now recognizes equivalent persona-assignment phrasing such as "act as," "take on the role of," or "become." This fixes a refusal that occurred when a role was assigned using non-exact phrasing (the "master ministry heads" issue).

Added a new General Research Capability, available under any role. Open-ended research requests are no longer treated as unsupported. This fixes the "not capable" refusal that occurred when a research request was combined with an unrecognized persona.
